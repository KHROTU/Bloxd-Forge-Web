Introduction
This privacy policy ("policy") will help you understand how ProduX ("us", "we", "our") uses and protects the data you provide to us when you visit and use bloxdforge.com ("website", "service").

We reserve the right to change this policy at any given time, of which you will be promptly updated. If you want to make sure that you are up to date with the latest changes, we advise you to frequently visit this page.
What User Data We Collect
When you visit bloxdforge.com, we may collect the following data:

  • Cookies to save your login status, authorize access to features, and ensure security.
  • Chat messages from "BloxdForge Agent" and "BloxdForge Coder" (stored for up to 24 hours for security purposes).
Notice: When you comment in the Workshop section using Disqus, your data is collected and managed by Disqus. We do not control or have access to user data collected by Disqus. Please review the Disqus Privacy Policy for more information.
Why We Collect Your Data
We collect your data for the following purposes:
  • To save your login status and provide seamless access to your account.
  • To authorize access to specific features and ensure only permitted users can utilize certain services.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our platform, protecting both user data and service functionality.
  • To monitor usage and prevent misuse of our services, ensuring a safe and fair environment for all users.
Safeguarding and Securing the Data
ProduX is committed to securing your data and keeping it confidential. We have implemented the latest technologies and software to prevent data theft, unauthorized access, and disclosure, ensuring that all the information we collect online is safeguarded.
Our Cookie Policy
We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are collected to save your login status, authorize access to specific features, and ensure the security of your account and our services.

Once you agree to allow our website to use cookies, you also agree to the use of data collected regarding your online behavior (such as analyzing web traffic, web pages you visit, and time spent on the site).

The data we collect by using cookies is used to customize our website to your gaming needs and to maintain secure access. After we use the data for statistical analysis or security purposes, the data is removed from our systems as appropriate.

Please note that cookies do not allow us to gain control of your computer in any way. They are strictly used to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not, so that we can provide a better and more secure gaming experience for you.

If you want to disable cookies, you can do it by accessing the settings of your internet browser. You can visit https://www.internetcookies.com, which contains comprehensive information on how to do this on a wide variety of browsers and devices.
Chat Message Storage
For security and quality assurance, chat messages from "BloxdForge Agent" and "BloxdForge Coder" are stored for up to 24 hours. This temporary storage helps us monitor interactions, ensure compliance with our policies, and protect users from misuse. After this period, chat data is securely deleted from our systems.

Visiting those websites is not governed by this privacy policy agreement. Make sure to read the privacy policy documentation of the website you go to from our website.
Restricting the Collection of your Personal Data
At some point, you might wish to restrict the use and collection of your personal data. You can achieve this by doing the following:
  • When you are filling out forms on the website, make sure to check if there is a box you can leave unchecked if you don't want to disclose your personal information.

ProduX will not lease, sell, or distribute your personal information to any third parties, unless we have your permission. We might do so if the law requires it. Your personal information will be used when we need to send you promotional materials, but only if you agree to this privacy policy.
