Our Cookie Policy

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are collected to save your login status, authorize access to specific features, and ensure the security of your account and our services.

Once you agree to allow our website to use cookies, you also agree to the use of data collected regarding your online behavior (such as analyzing web traffic, web pages you visit, and time spent on the site).

The data we collect by using cookies is used to customize our website to your gaming needs and to maintain secure access. After we use the data for statistical analysis or security purposes, the data is removed from our systems as appropriate.

Please note that cookies do not allow us to gain control of your computer in any way. They are strictly used to monitor which pages you find useful and which you do not, so that we can provide a better and more secure gaming experience for you.

If you want to disable cookies, you can do it by accessing the settings of your internet browser. You can visit https://www.internetcookies.com, which contains comprehensive information on how to do this on a wide variety of browsers and devices.
