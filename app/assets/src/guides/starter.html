Getting Started with BloxdForge

Here we will walk you through the essential steps to get started with BloxdForge, ensuring a smooth experience from installation to basic feature usage.

1: Zooming out

Please use Ctrl + to zoom in or Ctrl - to zoom out until Bloxdforge fits your screen perfectly.

2: Menu

In the menu, you will find various options, most of which are straightforward. However, the resolution option is a bit different. By setting it to 2, it lowers the game's resolution, making it ideal for low-end computers.

3: Tpack pin

At the top right corner, click the folder button. When asked to upload a folder, select the one containing your tpack. This will pin the folder to the menu. Press the chosen folder’s button to open it. Now, you can drag your tpack in the game whenever you want.

4: Keys?

Certain features, such as the Tpack editor, are accessible only with Keys. You can obtain these Keys for free in the Key section by watching some ads.

"Creator tab"

The Texture hub allows you to create and modify Tpacks. Here’s how to use.

1: Opening the Editor

The Tpack editor requires a key, which can be obtained from the 'Key' tab.

2: Menu

Upon entering the key, various options will appear in a sidebar. Clicking on these options will open windows that perform different functions.

3: Start Your Work

The first option is an editor specifically designed for bloxd.io.You can download textures from the GitHub repository, upload them into the editor, and begin editing right away.

Tip: Always save your textures. To avoid losing any progress.
